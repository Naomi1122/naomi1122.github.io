About Crime In Chicago


The background

The city of Chicago, Illinois is a world-famous tourist destination that attracts millions of tourists per year, yet it has one of the highest levels of crime in the country.

As tourist interest in the city rises up again after the pandemic while its crime rates continue to soar, more and more people will be interested to find out how to keep themselves safe while visiting one of America's most crime-ridden cities.

Our Mission

How can we help visitors to Chicago make better, more informed decisions about their safety and wellbeing?

As renting an Airbnb is one of the most convenient and affordable options available to travellers, this website displays the distribution of Airbnbs across the city along with the location-based crime data of Chicago from the previous month over an interactive map.

With the map functionalities we provide, visitors can cross reference both sets of data to understand the overall safety and risk levels of the city and determine an optimal location to stay in upon their visit.

Our Database

The data we will use is going to be collected from Chicago Data Portal, which is The City of Chicago's open data portal. The website is managed by the City of Chicago - Information Technology government department to ensure the authority of the data.
